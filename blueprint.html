Scoring Systems
Relevance (0-1)
Decides what shows on Today/Desktop.
Combines: recency, access frequency,
pinned status, project activity.
Decays over 30-day half-life.
Confidence (0-1)
How certain is this data?
Source quality, evidence amount,
classification certainty.
Drives Clarification queue.
